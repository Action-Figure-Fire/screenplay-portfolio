Synopsis

Tyler and Vivi are happily married — almost ten years, in fact. Everything seems perfect until the floodgates break open and their lives come crashing down. Their marriage hits a turning point when yet another argument leads to a wham-bam divorce filing.

But just before it's finalized, a job crisis and a mysterious missing $20,000 from their bank account halts everything — forcing the two to live together while legally redefining their relationship, all building to the commotion of their tenth anniversary.

Tyler has to decide: fight for his marriage, fight for his job, or stumble clumsily for both. Vivi navigates the unseasoned waters of the culinary world, discovering a version of herself her marriage never allowed. Both search for new lives with the help of their less-than-desirable — and comedic — best friends.

All the searching and failing leads them down a blind path of uncertainty. Tyler learns it's not about knowing when you have love — it's about knowing when to let it go. Vivi thinks she's found happiness in a world her marriage never let her experience. But will it last?

STUCK is not your ordinary love story. It's filled with action, comedy, twists and turns — all of the unexpected events life loves to throw at us just to see if we can stay standing.
