Synopsis

During a devastating U.S. depression, the political forces of America — backed by the powerhouse Winchester Finance — create a society where mandatory death at age 60 is the law. The result: an age of prosperity and youthfulness for the U.S. while the rest of the world rots in poverty, sickness, and violence.

Benjamin Braverly, the new CEO of Winchester, has a blood debt with the company. His father — the former CEO — was murdered along with his mother and sister just before the Quietus program launched, leaving Benjamin's uncle Caedyn to take the throne and raise him. Now at the helm, Benjamin is hell-bent on one thing: destroying the Resistance, the underground movement he believes killed his family.

But on the eve of Winchester's expansion into every major foreign nation, the Resistance reveals a truth that detonates Benjamin's entire worldview. Before he can process it, Winchester puts out a hit on their own CEO. Benjamin is forced to flee — hiding in the last place anyone would look: the very extermination facilities his company operates.

Transformed from corporate titan to fugitive rebel, Benjamin enlists a married mercenary couple with enough firepower to level a small country. With a dwindling but ignited militia, he must return to public life and expose the truth before Winchester's program goes global — no longer content with controlling America, but the entire world.
