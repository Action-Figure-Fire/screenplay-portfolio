## 📧 QUERY STRATEGY BY CONTACT

### For CAA/WME New Agents (Ally, Maia, Claire, Kelly, Liam):
**Subject:** Query: [TITLE] — [GENRE] | Previously Pursued by [CAA/WME]

"I'm reaching out because [Agency] previously expressed interest in my work. I'm currently seeking representation and would love to connect with you as you build your roster..."

*This is your NUCLEAR option. The fact that these same agencies wanted you before gives you instant credibility with their new agents.*

### For Paradigm/Verve/Innovative (New hires):
Standard query but emphasize: "Previously pursued by CAA and WME" prominently.

### For Managers:
More casual tone. Managers want to develop relationships, not just read scripts. Show your range (4 projects across genres) and your work ethic.
