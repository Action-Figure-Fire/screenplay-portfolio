## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **If an email bounces**, try alternate formats:
   - CAA alt: firstinitiallastname@caa.com (e.g., abrzezinski@caa.com)
   - WME alt: firstinitial.lastname@wmeagency.com
   - Paradigm alt: firstname.lastname@paradigmagency.com

2. **For management companies without emails**: Call the phone number, ask for the literary department, and ask for the best email to send a query. Receptionists will usually give it to you.

3. **LinkedIn DMs** work surprisingly well for junior agents. They're more likely to respond there than to cold emails.

4. **Do NOT send scripts with the query.** Only send the query email. Wait for them to request the script.

5. **Send 5-7 per day max.** You want to be able to track responses and follow up personally.
